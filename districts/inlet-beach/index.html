Inlet Beach Stormwater Priorities

Inlet Beach sits between the Gulf, Lake Powell, and the eastern gateway to 30A. A durable stormwater district should help keep runoff clean and slow, address known neighborhood drainage needs, and give residents a clear public process for deciding which projects come first.

Local priorities

What this district page tracks.

These are the durable local topics for Inlet Beach show notes, source links, video descriptions, podcast metadata, and future embeds.

  • Protect Lake Powell at the source, especially runoff from streets, roofs, parking areas, and redevelopment near the watershed.
  • Advance planned drainage work around Walton Magnolia Lane and Park Place so fixes are funded before repeated flooding becomes more expensive.
  • Coordinate Orange Street South improvements with drainage, beach access, pedestrian safety, and long-term maintenance.
  • Keep new and infill development tied to low-impact stormwater practices, especially near coastal dune lake protection areas.
  • Make Inlet Beach project priorities public, map-based, and benefit-based so residents can see what they are paying for and why.
