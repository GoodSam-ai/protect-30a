Corridor priorities

What this stormwater page tracks.

These topics anchor corridor-wide show notes, video descriptions, podcast metadata, and future embeds.

  • Drainage systems across 30A need maintenance, treatment capacity, and capital planning that follows water flow instead of political boundaries alone.
  • Rare coastal dune lakes need cleaner upstream runoff before stormwater carries sediment, nutrients, oils, trash, and bacteria toward lake basins and the Gulf.
  • Funding decisions should be public, project-based, benefit-based, and tied to stormwater work inside the area paying for it.
  • Road flooding, beach access pressure, redevelopment, and seasonal visitor traffic are connected infrastructure issues, not separate stories.
  • Residents need plain-language notices, maps, source documents, meeting agendas, and clear ways to act before major votes happen.
